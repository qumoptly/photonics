Where to Buy Output Couplers

Output coupler mirrors are partially reflecting mirrors used in lasers. They may be flat or curved, and generally have a well-defined reflectivity over some wavelength range for normal incidence. A wedge form may be necessary to avoid detrimental effects of parasitic reflections.
