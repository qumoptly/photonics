Where to Buy Plastic Optical Fibers

Plastic optical fibers may be made of PMMA (acrylic), polystyrene, polycarbonates or other polymer materials. They are used as multimode fibers for optical fiber communications over short or moderate distances, and as light pipes for various other applications.
