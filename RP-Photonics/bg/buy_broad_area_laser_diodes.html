Where to Buy Broad Area Laser Diodes

Broad area laser diodes, also called high-brightness or broad-stripe diodes, have an elongated emitter for higher output power – typically a few watts. They can be used for pumping solid-state lasers, for example.

Broad area lasers operate spatially and longitudinally multimode. Stripe widths from 60 μm to 400 μm are available to optimize beam structure and power for your application.
