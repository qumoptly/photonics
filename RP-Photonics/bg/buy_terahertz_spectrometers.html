Where to Buy Terahertz Spectrometers

Terahertz spectrometers can be used to spectrally analyzed terahertz radiation.

Related products: terahertz reflectometers, terahertz detectors

Related product category: detectors and measurements

TOPTICA Photonics has become one of the most successful suppliers of terahertz instrumentation world-wide. Ultrafast fiber lasers form the basis of the time-domain systems TeraFlash pro and TeraFlash smart, and precisely tunable diode lasers lie at the heart of the frequency-domain platform TeraScan.

Frequency-domain spectrometers are the preferred choice for applications requiring highest spectral resolution. Time-domain spectroscopy, on the other hand, offers the advantage of a very broad bandwidth: spectra acquired with state-of-the-art photoconductive switches cover frequencies up to 6 THz.
