Where to Buy Avalanche Photodiodes

An avalanche photodiode is a semiconductor-based photodetector with very high sensitivity, e.g. for detecting very weak signals and even for counting single photons (often in Geiger mode).

ESee also our encyclopedia article on avalanche photodiodes!
Related articles: photodiodes, photomultipliers
