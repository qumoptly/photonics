Where to Buy Radiation-resistant Fibers

For applications in space or in nuclear facilities, one requires fibers with have a substantially improved resistance to radiation. Fluorine-doped silica fibers, for example, can satisfy such requirements.

ERelated encyclopedia article: specialty fibers

Related products: fibers, silica fibers, fiber optics

Related product category: fiber optics
