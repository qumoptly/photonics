Where to Buy Pulse Stretchers

Pulse stretchers are used for temporally stretching laser pulses. They are needed in chirped-pulse amplification systems, for example, but also for certain methods of pulse characterization.

ESee also our encyclopedia article on pulse stretchers!
Related articles: pulse compression, chirped-pulse amplification, diffraction gratings

Related products: pulse compressors, pulse duration measurement devices

Related product category: various devices
