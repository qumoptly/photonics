Where to Buy Resonator Design Software

The design of laser resonators can be demanding, but can be supported with specialized software. Such software cannot only calculate the resonator modes for a given design, but also help to optimize designs according to certain performance targets. For example, one can optimize designs for certain mode sizes and minimum alignment sensitivity.
