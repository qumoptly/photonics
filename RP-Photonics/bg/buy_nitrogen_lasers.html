Where to Buy Nitrogen Lasers

Nitrogen lasers are pulsed ultraviolet lasers, mostly emitting at 337 nm. They typically emit microjoule pulses with nanosecond durations.

ERelated encyclopedia articles: gas lasers, ultraviolet lasers

Related products: ultraviolet lasers, gas lasers

Related product category: lasers and laser amplifiers
