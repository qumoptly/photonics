Where to Buy Patent Development

When a patent is developed based on a technical invention, it is essential to combine the expertise of technical and legal experts. Ideally, an experienced technical and scientific consultant works together with a patent attorney. Patent claims should not only cover the original idea of the invention, but also possible variations of it. A careful study of prior art is also essential.
