Where to Buy Beam Dumps

Various kinds of beam dumps can be used for temporarily or permanently blocking a laser beam – for example, for reasons of laser safety. High-power versions are fan-cooled or water-cooled.

ESee also our encyclopedia article on beam dumps!
Related article: laser safety

Related products: beam shutters, laser safety equipment and consulting

Related product category: optical components and devices
