Where to Buy Variable Reflectivity Mirrors

Variable reflectivity mirrors, having a radially dependent reflectance, can be used for realizing lasers with very large mode areas and nevertheless benign mode properties. They can also be used for variable optical attenuators.

ESee also our encyclopedia article on variable reflectivity mirrors!
Related article: unstable resonators

Related products: dielectric mirrors, mirrors

Related product category: optical components and devices
