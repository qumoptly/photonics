Where to Buy Dispersion Compensation Modules

In optical fiber communications, dispersion compensation modules (DCM, DCU) can be used for compensating the chromatic dispersion of, e.g., a long span of transmission fiber. Typically, such a module provides a fixed amount of dispersion, but tunable dispersion modules are also available.

ESee also our encyclopedia article on dispersion compensation!
Related articles: chromatic dispersion, dispersion compensation, optical fiber communications, fiber Bragg gratings, electronic dispersion compensation

Related products: dispersion management devices, chromatic dispersion measurement devices, optical fiber communication systems and devices

Related product categories: optical components and devices, telecom, various devices

The APE femtoControl is a compact motorized dispersion compensation unit for optimization of the duration of femtosecond laser pulses in the spectral range of Ti:sapphire lasers.

femtoControl compensates for material dispersion by applying the inverse amount of dispersion to the pulse. This is generated by a pair of prisms on motorized translation stages allowing continuous adjustment of the pulse length.

For example, femtoControl can help to achieve more crisp and clearer microscope images especially for multi-photon microscopy.
