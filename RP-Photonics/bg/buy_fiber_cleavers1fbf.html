Where to Buy Fiber Cleavers

With fiber cleavers, one can reproducibility prepared clean fiber ends as needed e.g. for splicing and connectorizing. Some cleavers are suitable only for perpendicular ends, but there are also angle cleavers for making angled fiber ends, suppressing effects of Fresnel reflections.

ESee also our encyclopedia article on fiber cleavers!
Related articles: cleaving of fibers, fiber joints, fiber optics

Related product category: fiber optics
