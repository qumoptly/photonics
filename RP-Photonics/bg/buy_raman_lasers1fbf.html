Where to Buy Raman Lasers

Raman lasers are lasers based on stimulated Raman scattering. They can be used to access wavelength regions which are difficult to reach directly with conventional lasers. For example, they can be used as yellow or orange laser sources.

The term Raman lasers is sometimes also used for laser applied in Raman spectroscopy.

ESee also our encyclopedia article on Raman lasers!
Related articles: Raman amplifiers, Raman scattering

Related products: Raman amplifiers, Raman crystals, yellow and orange lasers

Related product categories: lasers and laser amplifiers, nonlinear optics
