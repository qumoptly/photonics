Where to Buy Photonic Bandgap Fibers

Some optical fibers exhibit guidance of light based on a photonic bandgap, as can be obtained from a pattern of air holes. This guiding principle even allows for hollow-core fibers, where most of the light propagates in air.

Simulate pump absorption in double-clad fibers, study beam propagation in fiber couplers, light propagation in tapered fibers, analyze the impact of bending, cross-saturation effects in amplifiers, leaky modes, etc.
