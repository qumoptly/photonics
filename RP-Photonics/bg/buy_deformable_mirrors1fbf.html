Where to Buy Deformable Mirrors

Deformable mirrors are mirrors which can be deformed in operation (using certain electrically controlled actuators) in order to adjust their focusing properties or compensate beam distortions. They are mostly used for adaptive optics, e.g. for astronomic telescopes and some other imaging instruments.
