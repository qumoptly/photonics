Where to Buy Transition-metal-doped Gain Media

Transition-metal-doped laser gain media involve laser-active ions like Ti3+, Cr3+, Cr3+ and Cr4+. They generally exhibit a substantial gain bandwidth, usable for broad wavelength durability or for ultrashort pulse generation.

ESee also our encyclopedia article on transition-metal-doped gain media!

Related products: laser gain media, chromium-doped gain media, titanium-doped gain media
