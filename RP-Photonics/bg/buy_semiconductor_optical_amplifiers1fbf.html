Where to Buy Semiconductor Optical Amplifiers

Semiconductor optical amplifiers can provide amplification for telecom signals at 1.3 μm or 1.55 μm, for example. They are also useful for certain signal processing applications, such as channel translation in WDM systems. Tapered amplifiers can provide output powers of several watts.
