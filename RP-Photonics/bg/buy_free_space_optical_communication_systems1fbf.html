Where to Buy Free-space Optical Communication Systems

It is possible to transmit data with laser beams propagating over substantial distances, e.g. between buildings or even in space, e.g. between satellites. Over moderate distances, data rates of many Gbit/s are possible with a single beam.

ESee also our encyclopedia article on free-space optical communications!
Related article: optical data transmission

Related products: optical data transmission systems, eye-safe lasers

Related product categories: applications, telecom
