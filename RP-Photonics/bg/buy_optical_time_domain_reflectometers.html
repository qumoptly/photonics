Where to Buy Optical Time-domain Reflectometers

Optical time-domain reflectometers are instruments which are mostly used in optical fiber communications, for example for locating defective splices or components or for measuring distributed propagation losses. They work based on the measurement of reflected light short pulses.

ESee also our encyclopedia article on optical time-domain reflectometers!
Related article: distance measurements with lasers

Related products: optical sensors, laser rangefinders

Related product categories: detectors and measurements, applications
