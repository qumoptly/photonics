Where to Buy Periodically Poled Nonlinear Crystals

Periodically polled nonlinear crystals can be used for quasi-phase-matched nonlinear processes where phase matching would normally not be possible based on the original non-poled material. Through an appropriate choice of poling period, basically any nonlinear process can be quasi-phase-matched.

ESee also our encyclopedia article on quasi-phase matching!
Related articles: periodic poling, nonlinear frequency conversion, phase matching, quasi-phase matching

Related products: nonlinear crystal materials

Related product categories: materials, various devices

- PPLN waveguides: excellent performance, i.e. >50% overall efficiency. Fibered module >2 W at 780 nm ( 1560 nm SHG) is off-the-shelf. Other wavelengths are also available or could be designed upon request.
