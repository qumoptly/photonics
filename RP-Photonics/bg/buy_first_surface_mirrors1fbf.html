Where to Buy First Surface Mirrors

First surface mirrors (or front surface mirrors) have their reflecting coating on the front surface. That avoids various disadvantages of second surface mirrors, but also leads to higher sensitivity of the device. There are protected and enhanced versions with additional thin coatings.

ESee also our encyclopedia article on first surface mirrors!
