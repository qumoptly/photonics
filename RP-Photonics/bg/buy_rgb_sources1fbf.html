Where to Buy RGB Sources

Red, green and blue light, often in the form of laser beams, can be generated with various types of RGB sources. They may be based on gas lasers or solid-state lasers, for example.

ESee also our encyclopedia article on RGB sources!
Related article: nonlinear frequency conversion

Related products: red lasers, green lasers, blue lasers

Related product categories: lasers and laser amplifiers, nonlinear optics, vision, displays and imaging
