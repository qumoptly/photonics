Where to Buy Polarization Scramblers

Polarization scramblers can be used for making light unpolarized by rapidly changing the polarization state in a random manner. This can be useful for suppressing certain polarization effects, e.g. in long-distance optical fiber transmission systems.

ERelated encyclopedia article: polarization of laser emission

Related products: fiber-optic depolarizers, polarization controllers, polarizers

Related product category: various devices
