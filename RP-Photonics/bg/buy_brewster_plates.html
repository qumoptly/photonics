Where to Buy Brewster Plates

Brewster plates are played of some optical material with coplanar surfaces. They can be used for enforcing linear polarization of lasers, for example.

ESee also our encyclopedia article on Brewster plates!
Related articles: depolarization loss, polarization of laser emission, etalons

Related products: Brewster windows, optical flats

Related product category: optical components and devices
