Where to Buy Electro-optic Sampling Systems

Electro-optic sampling systems can be used to investigate high-frequency electronic circuits with extremely high temporal resolution. One can use the technique for ultrafast sampling oscilloscopes working up to frequencies of several terahertz.

ESee also our encyclopedia article on electro-optic sampling!
