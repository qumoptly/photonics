Where to Buy Thin-film Production Equipment

Thin-film devices can be fabricated with a variety of matters, including electron-beam deposition, ion-assisted deposition (IAD), ion beam sputtering (IBS) and advanced plasma reactive sputtering (APRS).

Related products: dielectric coatings, dielectric mirrors, anti-reflection coatings, rugate filters

Related product category: manufacturing equipment

10 suppliers for thin-film production equipment are listed in the RP Photonics Buyer's Guide. Both manufacturers and distributors can be registered.
