Where to Buy Optical Frequency Standards

An active optical frequency standard provides light with a highly stable optical frequency, which can be used for an optical clock, for example. There are also passive optical frequency standards, with which the optical frequency of a given light beam can be precisely analyzed.

Related products: optical clocks, narrow-linewidth lasers, single-frequency lasers, reference cavities
