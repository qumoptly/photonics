Fluctuations and Noise

  • noise sources: thermal noise, quantum noise, amplifier noise, vibrations
  • noise specifications, mathematical description, calculations
  • electronic and optical noise characterization and measurement (e.g. with spectrum analyzers or PC sampling cards; extensive know-how concerning spectral analysis, data acquisition and numerical processing)
  • specialized data processing: for example, developing software for calculating noise spectra from measured raw data
  • noise modeling (analytical and numerical techniques); effects of noise in components and systems
    (e.g. intensity and phase noise, timing jitter, carrier–envelope offset noise, impact in metrology, e.g. in interferometers)
  • noise suppression with feedback systems
  • photodetection: photodiodes and preamplifiers (e.g. transimpedance amplifiers); noise limits for sensitivity
