User Feedback

The users' feedback on this encyclopedia and also to the Photonics Spotlight is very welcome at RP Photonics, as it may trigger further improvements. For example, you may

  • indicate errors, inconsistencies or unclear explanations which you have found
  • suggest additional articles or additions to existing articles
  • suggest additional literature references

The simplest way to deliver feedback is to use the feedback form which is displayed below each article. This allows you to give anonymous feedback; of course, you may put your e-mail address into the comments field if you like to have an answer from the author.

You may also provide feedback via e-mail or telephone – see the contact page for details.
