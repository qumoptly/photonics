Where to Buy Deuterium Lamps

Deuterium arc lamps are broadband ultraviolet gas discharge lamps containing deuterium gas. They are mostly used in optical spectroscopy.

ERelated encyclopedia article: ultraviolet light

Related products: gas discharge lamps

Related product category: non-laser light sources
