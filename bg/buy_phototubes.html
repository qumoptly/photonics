Where to Buy Phototubes

Phototubes are vacuum tubes used as photodetectors. For some applications, they are prepared to photodiodes. For example, one may profit from the lower dark current (and thus lower noise equivalent power), the combination of large active area with a high detection bandwidth, the high dynamic range and high stability, e.g. under UV irradiation.
