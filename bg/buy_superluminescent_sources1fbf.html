Where to Buy Superluminescent Sources

Superluminescent sources emit broadband light, usually with high spatial coherence. Most common are superluminescent diodes, but they are also fiber-based ASE sources for higher output power levels.

ESee also our encyclopedia article on superluminescent sources!
Related articles: superluminescence, amplified spontaneous emission, superluminescent diodes, white light sources

Related products: superluminescent diodes, supercontinuum sources

Related product category: lasers and laser amplifiers
