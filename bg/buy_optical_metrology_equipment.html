Where to Buy Optical Metrology Equipment

Optical metrology is the science and technology of performing measurements with light – for example, distance measurements with lasers, frequency standards and optical clocks, measurements of optical powers, wavelengths and linewidths.

ESee also our encyclopedia article on optical metrology!
Related articles: frequency metrology, optical clocks, interferometers, distance measurements with lasers

Related products: optical metrology services, frequency metrology equipment, optical clocks, laser rangefinders, optical time-domain reflectometers

Related product categories: detectors and measurements, applications
