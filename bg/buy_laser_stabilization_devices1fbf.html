Where to Buy Laser Stabilization Devices

The output power, optical wavelength or other properties of lasers can be stabilized with suitable devices. For example, noise eaters suppress power fluctuations, and high-Q optical resonators can serve as frequency stabilization devices.

ESee also our encyclopedia article on stabilization of lasers!
Related articles: laser noise, intensity noise, phase noise, linewidth, noise eaters, injection locking, carrier–envelope offset

Related products: laser noise measurement equipment, noise eaters, laser synchronization devices

Related product categories: lasers and laser amplifiers, various devices
