Where to Buy Linewidth Measurement Equipment

The linewidth of lasers is relevant for various applications. Specialized measurement equipment can reliably measure laser linewidth and can contribute to comprehensive and helpful laser noise specifications. Different measurement techniques can be applied, e.g. spectrometers, interferometers, self-heterodyne measurement setups and beat note analysis.
