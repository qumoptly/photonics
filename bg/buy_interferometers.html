Where to Buy Interferometers

A wide range of interferometers is available for a large variety of applications e.g. in optical metrology, pulse characterization and holography.

ESee also our encyclopedia article on interferometers!
Related article: interferometers

Related products: Fabry–Perot interferometers, etalons, Fizeau interferometers, Twyman–Green interferometers, Gires–Tournois interferometers, white light interferometers, optical profilometers

Related product category: detectors and measurements
