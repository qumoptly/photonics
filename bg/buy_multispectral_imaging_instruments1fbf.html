Where to Buy Multispectral Imaging Instruments

Multispectral cameras provide images of a scene taken in multiple wavelength bands. They are used e.g. on airplanes and satellites for Earth monitoring, acquiring useful data e.g. for agricultural and environmental monitoring or for military surveillance.

ESee also our encyclopedia article on multispectral imaging!

Related products: hyperspectral imaging instruments, cameras

Related product categories: vision, displays and imaging
