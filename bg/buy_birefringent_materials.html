Where to Buy Birefringent Materials

Birefringent optical materials have a refractive index which depends on the polarization direction. They are used in polarizers, laser tuners and various other optical devices.

ESee also our encyclopedia article on birefringence!
Related articles: refraction, birefringent tuners, birefringent phase matching, polarization-maintaining fibers, Lyot filters

Related products: birefringent tuners

Related product category: materials
