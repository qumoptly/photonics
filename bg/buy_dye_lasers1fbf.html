Where to Buy Dye Lasers

Dye lasers are lasers with a dye solution as the gain medium. They offer broad wavelength durability in the visible, ultraviolet or infrared spectral region and are used for laser spectroscopy, for example.

ESee also our encyclopedia article on dye lasers!
Related articles: tunable lasers, titanium–sapphire lasers, ultrafast lasers, mode-locked lasers

Related products: tunable lasers
