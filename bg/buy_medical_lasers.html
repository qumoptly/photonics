Where to Buy Medical Lasers

Laser devices have found many applications in areas of medicine like dermatology, dentistry, ophthalmology, neurology and general surgery. They operate in different wavelength regions, with different power levels and pulse formats.

ESee also our encyclopedia article on medical lasers!

Related products: dermatology laser devices, ophthalmology equipment, alexandrite lasers, ruby lasers

Related product category: lasers and laser amplifiers
