Where to Buy Telecom Fibers and Cables

Optical fibers for telecom applications may be single-mode or multimode. Few-mode and multi-core fibers are used for mode division multiplexing. Both glass fibers and plastic optical fibers are available. Special versions may have increased or decreased effective mode areas or modified dispersion properties.
