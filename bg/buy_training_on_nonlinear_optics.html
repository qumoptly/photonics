Where to Buy Training on Nonlinear Optics

An in-house training course, delivered by an experience technical and scientific expert with well-developed didactical skills, can boost the competence of your team in specialized areas, profoundly improving its efficiency and creativity. You may learn, for example, on the physical principles of nonlinear frequency conversion, essential properties of nonlinear crystal materials and typical trade-offs and limitations in the design of frequency conversion devices.
