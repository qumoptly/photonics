Where to Buy Optical Displacement Sensors

Optical displacement sensors can be based on different measurement principles, for example on interferometry or on position-sensitive detectors. Some offer a very high spatial resolution.

ERelated encyclopedia article: fiber-optic sensors

Related products: laser rangefinders, fiber-optic sensors, optical sensing instruments

Related product categories: detectors and measurements, applications

10 suppliers for optical displacement sensors are listed in the RP Photonics Buyer's Guide. Both manufacturers and distributors can be registered.
