Where to Buy Multiple Element Lenses

Multiple element lenses are used for many applications, where a single lens could not achieve the required performance, for example in terms of aberrations. For example, microscope objectives, projection objectives and scanning lenses are normally made as multi-element lenses.

ERelated encyclopedia article: lenses

Related products: microscope objectives, projection objectives, scanning lenses

Related product category: optical components and devices
