Where to Buy Argon Ion Lasers

Argon ion lasers are powerful gas lasers, which typically generate multiple watts of optical power in a green or blue output beam with high beam quality.

ESee also our encyclopedia article on argon ion lasers!
Related article: gas lasers

Related products: gas lasers, krypton ion lasers, green lasers

Related product category: lasers and laser amplifiers
