Where to Buy Composite Laser Crystals

Composite laser crystals (or hybrid laser crystals) are laser crystals which have been fabricated by combining different parts. For example, there are laser roads where only the center region is doped, or where undoped regions are bonded to the ends. They can have certain advantages for laser design.
