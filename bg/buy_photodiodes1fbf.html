Where to Buy Photodiodes

Photodiodes of p–n and p–i–n type are available, based on different materials such as silicon, germanium and indium gallium arsenide. They differ substantially concerning wavelength regions, responsivity, active area, dark current and electrical capacitance.

ESee also our encyclopedia article on photodiodes!
Related articles: p–i–n photodiodes, photodetectors, velocity-matched photodetectors, avalanche photodiodes, metal–semiconductor–metal photodetectors, phototubes

Related products: p–i–n photodiodes, avalanche photodiodes, photodetectors, photodiode amplifiers, photodiode arrays, balanced photodetectors

Related product category: detectors and measurements
