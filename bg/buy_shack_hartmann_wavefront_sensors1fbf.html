Where to Buy Shack–Hartmann Wavefront Sensors

Hartmann–Shack wavefront sensors can measure wavefront shapes and even whole optical amplitude distributions e.g. of laser beams or star light. They are widely used in adaptive optics and in laser beam characterization.

ESee also our encyclopedia article on Shack–Hartmann wavefront sensors!
Related articles: beam profilers, beam quality

Related product categories: detectors and measurements, vision, displays and imaging
