Where to Buy Telecom Transmitters

An optical transmitter for telecom signals contain some light source, which may be a laser diode or a light-emitting diode for multimode operation. Transmitters differ in terms of maximum data rate, average power level and modulation format.

ERelated encyclopedia articles: optical data transmission, optical fiber communications

Related products: optical data transmission systems, optical fiber communication systems and devices

Related product category: telecom

30 suppliers for telecom transmitters are listed in the RP Photonics Buyer's Guide. Both manufacturers and distributors can be registered.
