Where to Buy Thermal Imaging Cameras

Thermal imaging cameras essentially provide spatially resolved temperature data, which are useful for example for assessing the energetic qualities of buildings or for finding defects in machinery. They may rely on bolometers or on semiconductor photodetectors with small bandgap energy. Some of them need to be cooled during operation for reaching a higher sensitivity.
