Where to Buy Laser Beam Characterization Instruments

Laser beams often need to be characterized in terms of beam shape profile and beam quality, or concerning optical power, pulse properties and optical spectrum. For such purposes, various kinds of beam profiles, beam quality measurement instruments, wavefront analyzers, optical power meters, spectrographs and other devices are available.

ESee also our encyclopedia article on laser beam characterization!
Related articles: beam profilers, beam quality, beam parameter product, M2 factor

Related products: beam profilers, wavefront analyzers, beam quality measurement devices

Related product category: detectors and measurements
