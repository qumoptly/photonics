Where to Buy Velocity-matched Photodetectors

Velocity-matched photodetectors are detectors with a particularly high detection bandwidth. They may be used in optical communications and for very high speed measurements.

ESee also our encyclopedia article on velocity-matched photodetectors!

Related product category: detectors and measurements
