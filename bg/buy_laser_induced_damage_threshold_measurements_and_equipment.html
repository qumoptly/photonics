Where to Buy Laser-induced Damage Threshold Measurements and Equipment

The laser-induced damage threshold of optical components such as laser mirrors is relevant for many applications. It should be assessed with specialized equipment, involving short laser pulses with well defined properties, and the results can be used to optimize optics fabrication.

ERelated encyclopedia articles: laser-induced breakdown, self-focusing

Related product categories: detectors and measurements, services and training
