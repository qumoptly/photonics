Where to Buy Mode-locked Lasers

Mode-locked lasers generate trains of picosecond of femtosecond pulses, with pulse durations between about 5 fs and 50 ps. They are mostly solid-state bulk or fiber lasers, but one also uses dye lasers and mode-locked laser diodes. Cavity dumping and/or amplification may be used for higher pulse energies.
