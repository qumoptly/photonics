Where to Buy Colorimeters

Colorimeters are instruments for measuring wavelength-dependent absorbance, which determines the color appearance of objects.

ESee also our encyclopedia article on colorimeters!

Related products: photometers, spectrophotometers, spectrometers

Related product category: detectors and measurements
