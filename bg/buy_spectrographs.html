Where to Buy Spectrographs

Spectrographs are instruments which can be used for measuring optical spectra, e.g. emission spectra of light sources. In contrast to scanning spectrometers, they contain a multi-channel photodetector (e.g. a photodiode array) for simultaneous recording of spectral components.
