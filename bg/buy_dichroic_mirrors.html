Where to Buy Dichroic Mirrors

Dichroic mirrors are mirrors which have substantially different reflection or transmission properties at two different wavelengths. For example, they can be used for end pumping of lasers, in lasers with intracavity frequency doubling, for resonant frequency doubling and in fluorescence microscopy.
