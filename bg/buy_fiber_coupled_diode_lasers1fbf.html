Where to Buy Fiber-coupled Diode Lasers

Various kinds of diode lasers (e.g. broad-stripe lasers and diode bars) are available where the output is coupled into an optical single-mode or multimode fiber. This is convenient, for example, for connecting pump diodes to diode-pumped lasers and fiber lasers or amplifiers.
