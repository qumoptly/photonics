Where to Buy Arrayed Waveguide Gratings

An arrayed waveguide grating is a (typically fiber-coupled) device which can separate or combine signals with different wavelengths. They are mainly applied in optical fiber communication systems, in particular in those based on multi-channel transmission with wavelength division multiplexing (WDM).
