Where to Buy Flash Lamps

Flash lands are gas discharge lamps which are operated in a pulsed mode. They can be used for the pumping of solid-state lasers, for example.

ESee also our encyclopedia article on flash lamps!
Related article: lamp-pumped lasers

Related products: arc lamps, pump chambers, lamp-pumped lasers

Related product category: non-laser light sources
