Where to Buy Laser Noise Measurement Equipment

There are different types of laser noise which sometimes needs to be assessed quantitatively: namely, intensity noise and phase noise of continuous-wave lasers, fluctuations of pulse parameters like energy and duration, timing jitter etc. The required equipment may include fast photodetectors, electronic spectrum analyzers, optical cross-correlators and computers including software.

ESee also our encyclopedia article on laser noise!
Related articles: noise specifications, intensity noise, phase noise, linewidth, Schawlow–Townes linewidth, stabilization of lasers

Related products: intensity noise measurement equipment, phase noise measurement equipment, timing jitter measurement devices, laser stabilization devices

Related product category: detectors and measurements
