Where to Buy Diffraction Gratings

Diffraction gratings are optical devices containing a periodic phase or amplitude structure. They may be used for grading spectrometers and monochromators or for the stretching and compression of ultrashort pulses.

ESee also our encyclopedia article on diffraction gratings!
Related articles: Bragg gratings, volume Bragg gratings, spectrometers, chromatic dispersion, pulse compression, pulse stretchers, spectral beam combining

Related products: Bragg gratings, fiber Bragg gratings, volume Bragg gratings, pulse compressors, pulse stretchers
