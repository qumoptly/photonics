Where to Buy Acousto-optic Deflectors

Acousto-optic deflectors are devices which can be used to deflect a laser beam in one direction by a variable angle, controlled by the frequency of an electrical signal.

ESee also our encyclopedia article on acousto-optic deflectors!

Related products: acousto-optic modulators

Related product categories: optical modulators, optical components and devices
