Where to Buy Pulsed Lasers

Different kinds of lasers can be used to generate light pulses with a wide range of pulse durations, energies and peak powers. They include Q-switched lasers and gain-switch lasers, and the term is also often used for ultrashort pulse lasers with pulse durations in the picosecond of femtosecond domain.
