Where to Buy Dispersion-shifted Fibers

Dispersion-shifted fibers are optical fibers with modified chromatic dispersion properties. They are mostly used for dispersion compensation or other kinds of dispersion management in optical fiber communication systems.

ESee also our encyclopedia article on dispersion-shifted fibers!
Related articles: chromatic dispersion, telecom fibers, specialty fibers

Related products: telecom fibers and cables, fiber optics

Related product categories: fiber optics, optical components and devices
