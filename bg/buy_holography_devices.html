Where to Buy Holography Devices

Various devices are required for holography, including narrow-linewidth lasers (continuous-wave or pulsed), beam splitters, mirrors and recording devices e.g. based on photographic emulsions.

ESee also our encyclopedia article on holography!

Related product categories: applications, vision, displays and imaging
