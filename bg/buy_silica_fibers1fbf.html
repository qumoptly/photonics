Where to Buy Silica Fibers

Silica fibers are the most common type of optical fibers. They can be made as single-mode of multimode fibers; there are also special versions which are photosensitive or radiation-resistant, for example.

ESee also our encyclopedia article on silica fibers!
Related article: telecom fibers

Related products: fibers, radiation-resistant fibers, photosensitive fibers, fiber optics
