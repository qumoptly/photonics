Where to Buy Tunable Lasers

Wavelength tuning of lasers is necessary for many laser applications, for example laser spectroscopy. It is achievable for a range of lasers, including laser diodes, various solid-state bulk and fiber lasers and dye lasers.

ESee also our encyclopedia article on tunable lasers!
Related articles: wavelength tuning, titanium–sapphire lasers, vibronic lasers, dye lasers, optical parametric oscillators, distributed Bragg reflector lasers, external-cavity diode lasers

Related products: wavelength tuning devices, lasers, dye lasers, titanium–sapphire lasers, external-cavity diode lasers, wavelength-swept lasers

Related product category: lasers and laser amplifiers

For the mid-IR region, APE offers the Carmina, a professional IR light-source for the integration with scattering SNOM and AFM-IR microscopes. The unique combination of 300 cm-1 broadband as well as 20 cm-1 narrowband emission covers the two complementary nanoscale IR techniques s-SNOM and AFM-IR. The outstanding high output power between 5 mW and 300 mW in combination with an extremely low noise level is addressing a wider range of applications in the near-field imaging & spectroscopy field.
