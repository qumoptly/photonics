Where to Buy Lamp-pumped Lasers

Flash lamp-pumped lasers can produce pulses with particularly high pulse energies. They can be used for laser marking and as medical, for example.

ESee also our encyclopedia article on lamp-pumped lasers!
Related articles: solid-state lasers, YAG lasers, diode-pumped lasers

Related products: gas discharge lamps, hollow cathode lamps, flash lamps, pump chambers, solid-state lasers

Related product category: lasers and laser amplifiers
