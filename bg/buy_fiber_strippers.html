Where to Buy Fiber Strippers

Different types of fiber strippers are tools for stripping the coating from the end of an optical fiber. This may be required before splicing or connectorization, for example.

ERelated encyclopedia articles: fibers, cleaving of fibers

Related products: fusion splicers, mechanical fiber splicing equipment, fiber cleavers, fiber optics

Related product category: fiber optics
