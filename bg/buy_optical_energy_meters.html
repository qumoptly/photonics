Where to Buy Optical Energy Meters

Optical energy meters are instruments for optical energy measurements, typically based on a pyroelectric or photodiode sensor. They can be used for measuring the performance of pulsed lasers, for example.

ESee also our encyclopedia article on optical energy meters!
Related article: optical power meters

Related products: optical power meters, pyroelectric detectors

Related product category: detectors and measurements
