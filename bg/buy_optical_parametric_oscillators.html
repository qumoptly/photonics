Where to Buy Optical Parametric Oscillators

Optical parametric oscillators (OPOs) are versatile wavelength-tunable light sources, operated either in continuous-wave mode or (more frequently) with short light pulses. They are used in areas like laser spectroscopy, but also for military applications.
