Where to Buy Marketing in Photonics

There are various methods and tools for the marketing of photonics products, for example buyer's guides, search engine marketing, banner ads, sponsored webinars, website optimization, printed materials, exhibitions and trade shows, e-mail campaigns and social media platforms. It depends very much on the type of product and supplier what combination of methods can be expected to be most effective and economical.
