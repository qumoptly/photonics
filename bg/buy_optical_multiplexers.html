Where to Buy Optical Multiplexers

Optical multiplexers can be used for combining multiple optical signals, for example in telecommunications. One distinguishes different kinds of multiplexing, namely wavelength division multiplexing, time division multiplexing, space division multiplexing, mode division multiplexing, etc.

ERelated encyclopedia article: wavelength division multiplexing

Related products: wavelength division multiplexing devices, add–drop multiplexers, time division multiplexing devices, mode division multiplexing devices

Related product categories: optical components and devices, telecom, various devices
