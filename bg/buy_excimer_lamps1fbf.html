Where to Buy Excimer Lamps

Excimer lamps (excilamps) are a special type of gas discharge lamps generating ultraviolet light. They can generate quasi-monochromatic UV light with particularly short wavelengths, are fairly efficient, and can have long lifetimes. Typical applications are printing, photolithography, UV curing of adhesives, surface cleaning and surface modification, ozone generation and sterilization.
