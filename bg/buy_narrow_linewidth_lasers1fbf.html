Where to Buy Narrow-linewidth Lasers

For various applications like laser spectroscopy, optical metrology, optical sensors and holography one requires lasers emitting light with a very narrow linewidth, i.e., with high temporal coherence. They are usually single-frequency lasers. In extreme cases, sub-Hertz linewidth values are achieved.
