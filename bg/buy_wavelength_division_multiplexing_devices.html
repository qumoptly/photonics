Where to Buy Wavelength Division Multiplexing Devices

The transmission capacity of optical fiber communication systems can be greatly increased with wavelength division multiplexing, i.e., with the simultaneous transmission of many signal channels at different wavelengths. Typically required devices are laser sources at different wavelengths, multiplexers and broadband fiber amplifiers.

Related articles: optical fiber communications, time division multiplexing

Related products: optical fiber communication systems and devices, gain equalization filters
