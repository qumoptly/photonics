Where to Buy Fiber Polishing Equipment

For particularly high surface quality and well defined angles, fiber ends sometimes need to be polished. Various types of equipment are available for that, including fine polishing and lapping sheets for use with fiber ferrules, rotating disk machinery and various accessories.

Related products: fiber cleavers, fiber cleaning equipment

Related product category: fiber optics

13 suppliers for fiber polishing equipment are listed in the RP Photonics Buyer's Guide. Both manufacturers and distributors can be registered.
