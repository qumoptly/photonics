Where to Buy Monochromators

A monochromator is a kind of optical bandpass filter, which is in most cases tunable concerning the center wavelength of the pass band and possibly also concerning the transmission bandwidth. It can be part of a spectrometer, spectrophotometers or spectrograph, for example. Many monochromators are based on a Czerny–Turner design or a variant thereof.
