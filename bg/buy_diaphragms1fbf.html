Where to Buy Diaphragms

Optical diaphragms are thin devices with a aperture at the center, which may be circular or non-circular and fixed or adjustable. They can be used in photography, for example.

ESee also our encyclopedia article on diaphragms!

Edmund Optics offers a wide range of iris diaphragms. They are ideal for adapting imaging systems for a number of different applications or environments that would otherwise be too bright by limiting the amount of light in an area to manageable levels. Some diaphragms may be mounted directly onto imaging components or may be positioned separately in front of light sensors, offering multiple implementation choices. A selection of sizes as well as mounting accessories is also available for a range of versatile options.
