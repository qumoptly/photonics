Where to Buy Optical Amplifiers

Optical amplifiers are devices for amplifying light beams. They can be based on the laser principle, but there are also amplifiers based on nonlinear processes.

ESee also our encyclopedia article on optical amplifiers!
Related article: fiber amplifiers

Related products: fiber amplifiers, erbium-doped fiber amplifiers, high-power fiber lasers and amplifiers, optical parametric amplifiers, Raman amplifiers, regenerative amplifiers, semiconductor optical amplifiers, tapered amplifiers, ultrafast amplifiers

Related product category: lasers and laser amplifiers
