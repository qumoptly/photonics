Where to Buy Optical Attenuators

Various types of optical attenuators are available with which light can be attenuated in a controlled manner. For examples, neutral density filters can provide wavelength-independent attenuation. There are also variable optical attenuators, providing an adjustable degree of attenuation. Some devices are of bulk optical type, and there are also fiber-optic attenuators.
