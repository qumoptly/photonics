Where to Buy Vertical Cavity Surface-emitting Lasers

VCSELs our monolithic surface-emitting semiconductor lasers, which usually emit a few milliwatts of single-frequency output. They are used in optical fiber communications, for gas sensing and in optical clocks, for example.

ESee also our encyclopedia article on vertical cavity surface-emitting lasers!
