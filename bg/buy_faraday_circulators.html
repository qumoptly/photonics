Where to Buy Faraday Circulators

Faraday circulators are available with bulk optics or as fiber-coupled devices. They can be used, for example, for integrating reflective devices like fiber Bragg gratings into transmission lines or ring laser resonators.

ERelated encyclopedia articles: Faraday isolators, Faraday rotators

Related products: Faraday isolators, Faraday rotators

Related product category: optical components and devices
