Where to Buy Coherence Measurement Devices

Various types of instruments are suitable for measuring temporal or spatial coherence of light, e.g. of laser beams. Measured quantities may e.g. be the coherence length of time or the optical bandwidth, or some correlation function.

ESee also our encyclopedia article on coherence!
Related articles: coherence time, coherence length, linewidth, interference, laser speckle, coherent beam combining

Related products: linewidth measurement equipment, interferometers

Related product category: detectors and measurements
