Where to Buy Adaptive Optics

Adaptive optics is a term for optical elements where the shape of optical wavefronts can be controlled, for example for minimizing beam distortions or optimizing image quality. They may be used for astronomy and in ophthalmology, for example.

Related products: deformable mirrors

Related product categories: optical components and devices, vision, displays and imaging
