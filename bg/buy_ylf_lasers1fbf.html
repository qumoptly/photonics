Where to Buy YLF Lasers

Most YLF lasers are based on Nd:YLF and emit either at 1047 nm or 1053 nm. They may be used instead of Nd:YAG lasers e.g. if the shorter wavelength is required.

ESee also our encyclopedia article on YLF lasers!
Related articles: laser crystals, neodymium-doped gain media, rare-earth-doped gain media, solid-state lasers

Related products: solid-state lasers, YAG lasers, vanadate lasers

Related product category: lasers and laser amplifiers
