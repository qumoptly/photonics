Where to Buy Photoconductive Detectors

Photoconductive detectors (photoresistors), are photodetectors which exhibit a reduction in electrical resistance caused by incoming light. They are based on intrinsic or extrinsic absorption of light in semiconductors. Some of them (e.g. MCT detectors) offer a good infrared response.
