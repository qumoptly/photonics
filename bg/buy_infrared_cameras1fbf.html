Where to Buy Infrared Cameras

Mid-infrared cameras can be used for surveillance purposes, for example. They may be combined with infrared illuminators. Cameras for longer wavelengths (mid-IR) are also used as a thermal imaging cameras.

ESee also our encyclopedia article on infrared cameras!

Related products: thermal imaging cameras, cameras

Related product categories: detectors and measurements, vision, displays and imaging
