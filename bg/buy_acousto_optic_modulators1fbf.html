Where to Buy Acousto-optic Modulators

Acousto-optic modulators (AOM) are devices which can be used for controlling the power of a laser beam with an electrical drive signal.

ESee also our encyclopedia article on acousto-optic modulators!
Related articles: Q switching, Q-switched lasers, optical modulators, electro-optic modulators

Related products: optical modulators, acousto-optic frequency shifters, acousto-optic frequency shifters, acousto-optic deflectors, electro-optic modulators

Related product categories: optical modulators, optical components and devices

Check List for Acousto-optic Modulators

Click here to see a check list before buying such products!
  • Is the aperture sufficiently large for the laser beam?
  • Is the diffraction efficiency high enough?
  • How about the optical losses?
  • For Q-switched lasers: is the damage threshold of the modulator high enough? Is the switching sufficiently fast?
  • What modulator driver can I use? How about cables and connectors?
  • Are the outer dimensions small enough to integrate the modulator into my optical setup?

In case that you need additional advice, consultancy from RP Photonics may be the solution.
