Where to Buy Photonic Crystal Fibers

Photonic crystal fibers are microstructure fibers, containing tiny air holes along the fiber axis. A wide range of designs as possible, leading to remarkable guiding properties, for example to endlessly single-mode fibers, fibers with anomalous dispersion even in the visible spectral region, and hollow-core fibers. There are also active fibers for lasers and amplifiers, e.g. double-clad fibers with air cladding.
