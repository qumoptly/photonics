Where to Buy RF Drivers

Radiofrequency drivers are required as accessories for various photonic devices such as acousto-optic and electro-optic modulators. Some drivers simply generate a sinusoidally oscillating voltage with fixed amplitude and frequency, while others can be modulated in various ways.

Related products: Pockels cell drivers, acousto-optic modulators, electro-optic modulators

Related product categories: optical modulators, various devices

19 suppliers for RF drivers are listed in the RP Photonics Buyer's Guide. Both manufacturers and distributors can be registered.
