Where to Buy Infrared Viewers

Infrared viewers are often handheld devices with which infrared radiation can be converted to visible images. This is useful, for example, for work in laser labs.

ESee also our encyclopedia article on infrared viewers!
Related articles: infrared light, laser viewing cards

Related products: cameras

Related product categories: detectors and measurements, vision, displays and imaging
