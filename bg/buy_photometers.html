Where to Buy Photometers

Photometers are instruments for measuring light properties such as irradiance or illuminance, or optical properties of things. Devices for radiometry measure quantities like irradiance and radiant flux, while photo meters for photometry may measure illuminance and total luminous flux.

ESee also our encyclopedia article on photometers!
Related article: optical metrology

Related products: photodetectors, optical power meters, colorimeters

Related product category: detectors and measurements
