Where to Buy Balanced Photodetectors

Balanced photodetectors are suitable for sensitively measuring difference in optical powers between two inputs while largely suppressing common noise. Possible applications are sensitive absorption measurements and optical heterodyne measurements.

ESee also our encyclopedia article on balanced photodetection!

Related products: photodetectors, photodiodes

Related product category: detectors and measurements
