Where to Buy Fiber-optic Networks

Fiber-optic networks can be used as local area networks (LAN), metropolitan area networks (MAN) or wide area networks (WAN). Some of them are active, containing active components like fiber amplifiers, while others are passive.

ESee also our encyclopedia article on fiber-optic networks!
Related articles: optical fiber communications, fiber-optic links

Related products: fiber-optic links, optical fiber communication systems and devices, optical data transmission systems

Related product categories: fiber optics, telecom
