Where to Buy Time-of-flight Measurement Devices

Devices like laser rangefinders are based on time-of-flight measurements with high temporal resolution for measuring distances. They often involve lasers for generating short light pulses in conjunction with fast photodetectors.

ESee also our encyclopedia article on time-of-flight measurements!
