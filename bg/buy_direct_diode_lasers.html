Where to Buy Direct Diode Lasers

Direct diode lasers are laser devices where the output of laser diodes is directly used for an application like laser material processing. Compared with diode-pumped solid-state lasers, this approach has substantial advantages in terms of cost and power conversion efficiency, but is more limited in terms of beam quality and not suitable for the generation of intense pulses.
