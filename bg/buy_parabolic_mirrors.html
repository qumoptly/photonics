Where to Buy Parabolic Mirrors

Mirrors with a parabolic shape exhibits the lowest possible aberrations for certain optical purposes, such as tightly focusing a collimated laser beam. Some are made as off-axis reflectors, where the focal point is outside the incoming beam.
