Where to Buy High Brightness Laser Diodes

High brightness laser diodes are laser diodes with a particularly high radiance (brightness). They are needed for pumping of solid-state bulk and fiber lasers and for various other applications.

ESee also our encyclopedia article on high brightness laser diodes!

Related product category: lasers and laser amplifiers
