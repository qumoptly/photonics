Where to Buy Lamp Power Supplies

A wide range of power supplies is available for operating a large variety of lamps. Particularly sophisticated power supplies are required for high-power gas discharge lamps – both for arc lamps and flash lamps.

ESee also our encyclopedia article on lamp power supplies!

Related product category: non-laser light sources
