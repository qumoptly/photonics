Where to Buy Neodymium-doped Gain Media

Various crystalline and glass materials, also including optical fibers, are available with neodymium doping for laser operation or amplification. They are mostly used for the 1-μm spectral region, but there are also other laser lines e.g. around 1.3 μm and 0.95 μm.
