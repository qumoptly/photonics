Where to Buy Microlasers

Microlasers may be understood as miniature lasers with sub-millimeter dimensions, which can be used in integrated optics. However, the term is also used for somewhat larger miniature lasers, at being much more compact than usual lasers with similar performance characteristics.

ERelated encyclopedia articles: microchip lasers, monolithic solid-state lasers

Related products: microchip lasers

Related product category: lasers and laser amplifiers
