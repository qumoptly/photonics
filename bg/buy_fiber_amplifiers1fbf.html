Where to Buy Fiber Amplifiers

Fiber amplifiers are optical amplifiers based on optical fibers as gain media. High-power amplifiers can be used for laser material processing, for example, while lower-power amplifiers are common in optical fiber communications.

ESee also our encyclopedia article on fiber amplifiers!
Related articles: erbium-doped fiber amplifiers, high-power fiber lasers and amplifiers, rare-earth-doped fibers, double-clad fibers, gain equalization, ultrafast amplifiers, fiber simulation software

Related products: optical amplifiers, fiber optics, gain equalization filters

Related product categories: fiber optics, lasers and laser amplifiers
