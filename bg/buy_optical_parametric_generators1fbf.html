Where to Buy Optical Parametric Generators

Optical parametric generators are basically parametric amplifiers without a signal input, producing short or ultrashort pulses in wide wavelength regions.

ESee also our encyclopedia article on optical parametric generators!
Related articles: optical parametric amplifiers, parametric fluorescence, optical parametric oscillators

Related products: optical parametric amplifiers

Related product category: nonlinear optics
