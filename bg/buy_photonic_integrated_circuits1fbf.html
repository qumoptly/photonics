Where to Buy Photonic Integrated Circuits

Photonic integrated circuits (planar lightwave circuits, integrated optoelectronic devices) are devices on which several or even many optical (and often also electronic) components are integrated. They are applied in telecom systems, for example.

ESee also our encyclopedia article on photonic integrated circuits!
Related articles: integrated optics, silicon photonics, optical fiber communications

Related products: silicon photonics, planar waveguides

Related product category: various devices
