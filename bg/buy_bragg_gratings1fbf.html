Where to Buy Bragg Gratings

Bragg gratings are available in the form of volume Bragg gratings and fiber Bragg gratings. In any case, they reflect only specific wavelengths, e.g. for applications in optical filters.

ESee also our encyclopedia article on Bragg gratings!
Related articles: Bragg mirrors, volume Bragg gratings, fiber Bragg gratings

Related products: diffraction gratings, fiber Bragg gratings, volume Bragg gratings

Related product category: optical components and devices
