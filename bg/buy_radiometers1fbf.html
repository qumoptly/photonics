Where to Buy Radiometers

Radiometers are instruments for measuring radiometric quantities such as the radiant energy, energy density, radiant flux, spectral flux, radiance, irradiance and others.

ESee also our encyclopedia article on radiometry!

Related products: spectroradiometers, photometers

Related product category: detectors and measurements
