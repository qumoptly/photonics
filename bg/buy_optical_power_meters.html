Where to Buy Optical Power Meters

Optical power meters may be based on photodiodes with high sensitivity or on thermopiles for operation at higher power levels. Models for high powers can be air-cooled or water-cooled.

ESee also our encyclopedia article on optical power meters!
Related articles: optical power monitors, photodetectors

Related products: optical energy meters, optical power monitors, photometers

Related product category: detectors and measurements
