Where to Buy Flow Tubes

Flow tubes are glass tubes for channeling cooling water around a laser rod and lamp in a lamp-pumped laser.

ERelated encyclopedia article: lamp-pumped lasers

Related products: flash lamps, arc lamps, lamp-pumped lasers

Related product category: various devices

5 suppliers for flow tubes are listed in the RP Photonics Buyer's Guide. Both manufacturers and distributors can be registered.
