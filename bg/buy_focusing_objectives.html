Where to Buy Focusing Objectives

Focusing objectives can be used for various applications, where a laser beam needs to be tightly focused. Some of than have a substantially enhanced power handling capability, for example compared with that of microscope objectives.

Related products: scanning lenses, laser material processing devices

Related product category: optical components and devices
