Numerical Pulse Propagation

RP ProPulse can simulate pulse propagation in many devices:

  • in the resonators of actively or passively mode-locked lasers
  • in synchronously pumped OPOs
  • in optical fibers, e.g. for pulse compression or soliton studies

Further features:

  • interactive pulse display
  • great flexibility due to scripting
  • comprehensive documentation
  • competent technical support

Use RP ProPulse to acquire a full understanding of your devices!
