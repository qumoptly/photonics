Company Description

Gentec Electro-Optics has a long history in the laser measurement field. With a 40 year track record of innovation and providing quality solutions for laser power and energy measurement applications from the factory to the hospital and laboratory, Gentec-EO stands ready to serve you now and in the future.

Products

ProductEncDescription
beam dumps E

The BD Series beam dumps are rugged and easy-to-use, simply plug the water-cooling and you’re ready to go! Like our high power HP Series, these beam dumps have a highly resistant absorber that can withstand 4 kW or even 12 kW in continuous mode. Their very large aperture of 100 mm in diameter accommodates even the largest beams.

beam profilers E

When it comes to characterizing a laser beam in the UV to near-IR range, a beam profiler is the go-to solution.

Thanks to its unique combination of high pixel density and large sensor size, the BEAMAGE series of laser beam diagnostics instruments has the double advantage of accurately characterising both very small beams of only a few tens of microns to larger beams of several millimeters in width, thus effectively covering most applications in one package.

The simple software is highly intuitive and yet includes many great functions, useful both for basic or advanced users. Furthermore, the calculations done by the software are ISO-compliant and give the user the most accurate beam characterization available, all this in a lightweight environment that doesn’t take days to fully master.

beam quality measurement devices E

The BEAMAGE-M2 is an automated laser beam quality measurement system. It is the only M2 system on the market equipped with a complete set of 50-mm optics. Also, the sensor is 11.3 × 11.3 mm.

Two beam-steering mirrors are included for quick and easy alignment of your laser into the system. The internal mirrors are factory-aligned and the pre-set height also simplifies the alignment.

The low-profile ingenious mechanics make it easy to fit the device on any optical table.

The calculations are fully compliant to the ISO 11146 and 13694 standards.

Make a complete, ISO-compliant measurement in only 20 seconds with the ROI feature, and in less than a minute with full-frame acquisition.

In the easy-to-navigate software, both automatic and manual settings are available, so data points can be added or removed even after an automatic scan is completed.

beam splitters E

Gentec Electro-Optics offers beam splitters used as optical attenuators for measurements on high-power laser beams.

cameras E

Gentec Electro-Optics offers a variety of CMOS camera-based beam profilers, covering wavelength ranges from the ultraviolet to the infrared (with a phosphor coating). We also have a device for large laser beams, containing a fiber-optic taper.

See also our overview on laser beam diagnostics.

infrared detectors E

Gentec Electro-Optics has pyroelectric thermal detectors with active areas from 1 to 9 mm diameter. They are suitable for room temperature operation with broad spectral response, high sensitivity and high speed (sub-nanosecond response times).

integrating spheres E

Gentec Electro-Optics has integrating spheres for laser energy measurements and for power measurements. Instruments for up to 20 mJ energy or 9 W continuous-wave power are available, but also photodiode-based instruments with high sensitivity and speed.

laser beam characterization instruments E

Gentec Electro-Optics offers a variety of camera-based instruments. This includes compact laser beam profilers and more complex instruments for measuring M2 beam quality factor by automatically analyzing the beam propagation. Besides, we offer optical power meters and various accessories.

optical attenuators E

Gentec Electro-Optics offers optical attenuators for measurements on high-power laser beams.

optical energy meters E

Gentec Electro-Optics offers accurate optical energy meters. We have a variety of laser energy measurement heads, covering a wide range of pulse energies from sub-millimoules to 250 J. These can be combined with a great selection of displays and PC interfaces.

optical power meters E

Gentec Electro-Optics offers a variety of laser power meters, ranging from ultracompact photodiode-based devices for microwatt power levels to thermal detectors for powers up to 100 kW. We have a wide range of power measurement heads which can be combined with a great choice of displays and PC interfaces.

photodetectors E

Gentec Electro-Optics offers a great range of power detectors based on silicon or germanium photodiodes for powers up to 750 mW.

photodiodes E

Gentec Electro-Optics offers a great range of power detectors based on silicon or germanium photodiodes for powers up to 750 mW.

position-sensitive detectors E

Gentec Electro-Optics offers measurement systems for laser beam pointing based on large area pyroelectric quadrant detectors. The instruments respond to laser beams from the UV to the far IR and can be used even in the terahertz region. Versions for pulsed sources, CW and high repetition rate (quasi-CW) operation are available.

pyroelectric detectors E

Gentec Electro-Optics offers a selection of pyroelectric measurement heads for measuring high-energy laser pulses with up to 3.9 J, and various compatible displays and PC interfaces. There are also pyroelectric power detectors. Discreate pyroelectric thermal detectors are also available.

terahertz detectors E

Gentec Electro-Optics has a range of pyroelectric-based terahertz measurement heads and terahertz powermeters as well as suitable displays and PC interfaces. Whether you prefer to get your measurement on a large touch screen color LCD display or on your computer through USB/RS-232 connectivity, we have it all covered.
