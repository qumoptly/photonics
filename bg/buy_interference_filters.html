Where to Buy Interference Filters

Interference filters are optical filters which are based on interference effects. They are mostly dielectric multilayer devices and also include resonant devices such as Fabry–Pérot filters.

ESee also our encyclopedia article on interference filters!

Related products: optical filters, Fabry–Perot interferometers

Related product category: optical components and devices
