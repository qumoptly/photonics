Where to Buy Microlens Arrays

Microlens arrays are linear or two-dimensional arrays of microlenses. They have applications like Shack–Hartmann wavefront sensors and beam homogenizers.

ESee also our encyclopedia article on microlens arrays!
Related articles: microlenses, lenses

Related products: microlenses, Shack–Hartmann wavefront sensors, beam homogenizers

Related product category: optical components and devices

NIL Technology provides a wide range of both standard and custom microlens arrays with a broad range of lens configurations and spherical or aspherical lens shapes. We can offer both one-dimensional and two-dimensional arrays. We also have lens configurations as hexagonal and square configurations, as well as random, with options of close packed lenses or with gaps between the lenses.

We have a variety of masters with concave or convex microlens arrays for use in production of polymer microlens arrays by hot-embossing, UV replication or injection molding.

The masters are offered in fused silica and nickel. Lens depth/height and lens width can be controlled independently, so any parabolic lens shape can be realized on the stamps. Polymer replications with convex and concave lens arrays can also be provided.

We also have a wide selection of microlens standard stamps.
