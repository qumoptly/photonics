Where to Buy Bit Error Rate Testers

Bit error rate testers can measure the bit error rate of communications systems and are well defined conditions. They can be used for identifying malfunctions and for research and development purposes.

ESee also our encyclopedia article on bit error rate!
Related articles: optical data transmission, optical fiber communications

Related products: optical data transmission systems

Related product categories: detectors and measurements, telecom
