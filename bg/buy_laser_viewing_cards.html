Where to Buy Laser Viewing Cards

Laser viewing cards are frequently used in laser laboratories and industrial settings for checking the positions of infrared laser beams. They may work based on phosphorescence, upconversion fluorescence, or the direct generation of fluorescence light. There are also thermochromic materials.

ESee also our encyclopedia article on laser viewing cards!
Related articles: infrared viewers, laser beams, infrared light

Related products: infrared-to-visible converters, infrared viewers

Related product categories: detectors and measurements, various devices
