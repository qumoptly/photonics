Where to Buy Titanium-doped Gain Media

Titanium (Ti3) ions can be used as laser-active ions. The by far most prominent Ti-doped gain medium is Ti3:sapphire, used for ultrafast lasers and amplifiers as well as for tunable lasers.

ERelated encyclopedia articles: laser crystals, transition-metal-doped gain media, titanium–sapphire lasers

Related products: laser gain media, transition-metal-doped gain media

Related product category: materials
