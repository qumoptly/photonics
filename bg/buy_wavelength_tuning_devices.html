Where to Buy Wavelength Tuning Devices

Tunable lasers can contain various kinds of wavelength tuning devices, for example Lyot filters (birefringent tuners), etalons and diffraction gratings.

ESee also our encyclopedia article on wavelength tuning!
Related articles: tunable lasers, distributed Bragg reflector lasers, optical filters, Lyot filters, birefringent tuners

Related products: birefringent tuners, Lyot filters

Related product category: various devices
