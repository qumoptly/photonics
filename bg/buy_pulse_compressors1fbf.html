Where to Buy Pulse Compressors

Linear (dispersive) pulse compressors can be used to compress pulses which are already broadband. There are also devices which include a nonlinear element for spectral broadening.

ESee also our encyclopedia article on pulse compression!
Related articles: ultrashort pulses, spectral phase, pulse propagation modeling, pulse duration, dispersion compensation, self-phase modulation, adiabatic soliton compression, dispersive mirrors

Related products: pulse duration measurement devices

Related product category: various devices
