Where to Buy Fiber-optic Polarizers

Fiber-optic polarizers transmit only light with a certain linear polarization direction. Their connecting fibers may be polarization-maintaining or standard single-mode. Fiber polarizers are required for fiber-optic networks, e.g. for polarization monitoring and control, and in fiber lasers and amplifiers.

ERelated encyclopedia article: polarizers

Related products: polarizers, fiber polarization controllers, fiber optics, fiber-optic depolarizers

Related product category: fiber optics
