Where to Buy Frequency Comb Sources

Highly stabilized ultrashort pulse lasers can generate accurate frequency combs for applications in optical time and frequency metrology, also in precision spectroscopy.

ESee also our encyclopedia article on frequency combs!
Related articles: frequency metrology, optical spectrum, beat note, carrier–envelope offset, optical clocks, optical clockworks, titanium–sapphire lasers, optical heterodyne detection

Related products: frequency metrology equipment, carrier–envelope offset measurement and stabilization

Related product category: detectors and measurements
