Where to Buy Fusion Splicers

Fusion splicers can reliably produce very accurate and low-loss joints between optical fibers. A substantial degree of automation allows for rapid and particularly reproducible high-quality splicing. Some instruments are flexible for use with non-standard fibers.

ESee also our encyclopedia article on fusion splicing of fibers!
Related articles: fiber joints, cleaving of fibers

Related products: mechanical fiber splicing equipment, fiber cleavers, fiber optics

Related product category: fiber optics
