Where to Buy Supercontinuum Sources

Supercontinent sources produce light with very broad optical spectra. They may operate continuously or with high repetition rate pulse trains. Their high spatial coherence can be highly useful in applications like optical coherence tomography, fluorescence microscopy, flow cytometry and the characterization of optical devices.
