Where to Buy Microchannel Plate Detectors

Microchannel plate detectors can be used for a wide range of electromagnetic radiation, including visible, infrared and ultraviolet light, X-rays, charged particles and neutrons. They employ electron multiplication for reaching a very high sensitivity.
