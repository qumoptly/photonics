Where to Buy Thermoelectric Coolers

Thermoelectric coolers (Peltier coolers) can be used for cooling and temperature stabilization, for example in the context of laser diodes or nonlinear crystals. They can also be used for heating just by reversing the direction of current flow.

Related products: laser cooling units

Related product category: various devices
