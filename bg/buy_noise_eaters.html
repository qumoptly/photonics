Where to Buy Noise Eaters

A noise eater is a device made for reducing the intensity noise in a laser beam. It works by applying a variable attenuation, which is controlled according to the intensity fluctuations of the laser.

ESee also our encyclopedia article on noise eaters!
Related articles: laser noise, intensity noise, stabilization of lasers, optical attenuators

Related products: intensity noise measurement equipment, laser stabilization devices

Related product category: various devices
