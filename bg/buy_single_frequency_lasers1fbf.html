Where to Buy Single-frequency Lasers

Various kinds of single-frequency lasers can generate light with a very narrow linewidth. Some of them are suitable for long-term stable operation with high frequency stability and no mode hops. Possible applications are in interferometry (e.g. holography), laser spectroscopy (e.g. LIDAR) and optical communications. High-power single-frequency sources are required for applying coherent beam combining techniques.
