Where to Buy Quantum Cascade Lasers

Quantum cascade lasers are semiconductor based laser sources a meeting in the mid-infrared or in the terahertz region. They can be used for infrared absorption spectroscopy of trace gases, for example.

ESee also our encyclopedia article on quantum cascade lasers!
Related articles: semiconductor lasers, infrared light, mid-infrared laser sources, terahertz radiation, laser spectroscopy

Related products: mid-infrared laser sources, semiconductor lasers

Related product category: lasers and laser amplifiers
