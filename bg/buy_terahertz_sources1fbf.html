Where to Buy Terahertz Sources

Terahertz radiation can be generated for example in certain gas lasers, with high-frequency microwave technology, free-electron lasers and synchrotrons, difference frequency generation or optical rectification in nonlinear crystals or with photoconductive switches.

ESee also our encyclopedia article on terahertz sources!

Related products: terahertz crystals, terahertz detectors

Related product category: various devices

TOPTICA’s terahertz sources employ photoconductive antennas, which convert near-infrared laser radiation to terahertz light. TOPTICA offers modules both for time-domain and frequency-domain applications.

All of TOPTICA’s photoconductive antennas come equipped with a silicon lens and SM/PM fiber pigtail, and can be integrated in the terahertz platforms TeraScan, TeraFlash and TeraSpeed.
