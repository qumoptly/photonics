Where to Buy Fiber-optic Sensors

Fiber-optic sensors (optical fiber sensors) are fiber-based instruments for sensing some quantity, typically temperature or mechanical strain, but sometimes also displacements, vibrations, pressure, acceleration, rotations or concentrations of chemical species.

ESee also our encyclopedia article on fiber-optic sensors!
Related article: fiber Bragg gratings

Related products: optical sensors, optical sensing instruments, optical displacement sensors, optical temperature sensors, optical tilt sensors

Related product categories: detectors and measurements, fiber optics
