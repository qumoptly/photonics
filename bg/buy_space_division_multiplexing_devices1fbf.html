Where to Buy Space Division Multiplexing Devices

Space division multiplexing (SDM) is considered to be a major opportunity for further rising the transmission capacities of optical fiber communication systems. It can work with various kinds of fibers, including multi-core fibers and few-mode fibers. Components required for SDM include various kinds of fibers, spatial multiplexers, adapted fiber amplifiers.

ESee also our encyclopedia article on space division multiplexing!

Related products: optical fiber communication systems and devices, mode division multiplexing devices

Related product category: telecom
