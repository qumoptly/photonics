Where to Buy Alignment Lasers

Alignment lasers are lasers which are optimized for alignment purposes. They are available as dot lasers, line lasers and cross laser, for example, and with different wavelengths mostly in the visible region.

ESee also our encyclopedia article on alignment lasers!
Related article: laser pointers

Related products: laser pointers, laser trackers, optical alignment services

Related product categories: lasers and laser amplifiers, applications
