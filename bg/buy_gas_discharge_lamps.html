Where to Buy Gas Discharge Lamps

Gas discharge lamps are simple and robust light sources for various spectral regions, used for example for illumination purposes, pumping lasers and for spectroscopy.

ESee also our encyclopedia article on gas discharge lamps!
Related article: fluorescent lamps

Related products: arc lamps, flash lamps, spectral lamps, deuterium lamps, hollow cathode lamps, lamp-pumped lasers

Related product category: non-laser light sources

30 suppliers for gas discharge lamps are listed in the RP Photonics Buyer's Guide. Both manufacturers and distributors can be registered.
