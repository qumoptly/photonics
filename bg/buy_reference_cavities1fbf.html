Where to Buy Reference Cavities

Highly stable optical resonators can serve as reference cavities, i.e., as short-term frequency standards, as required for instruments like optical clocks.

ESee also our encyclopedia article on reference cavities!
Related articles: optical frequency standards, frequency metrology

Related products: optical frequency standards, optical clocks

Related product categories: detectors and measurements, various devices
