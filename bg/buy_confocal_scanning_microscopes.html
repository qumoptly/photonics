Where to Buy Confocal Scanning Microscopes

Confocal scanning microscopes are a kind of laser microscopes which can be used to produce 3D images with very high spatial resolution.

ESee also our encyclopedia article on confocal scanning microscopes!

Related products: laser microscopes, fluorescence microscopes

Related product categories: applications, vision, displays and imaging
