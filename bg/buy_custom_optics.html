Where to Buy Custom Optics

Various suppliers offer either custom optical elements or custom optical assemblies and systems, e.g. with extra-high precision or special properties. Compared with stock optics, such solutions are often more expensive, but not in all cases.

ESee also our encyclopedia article on custom optics!

Related products: lenses, prisms, mirrors, diffraction gratings, diaphragms, superpolished optics

Related product category: optical components and devices
