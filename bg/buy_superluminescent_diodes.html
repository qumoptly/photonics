Where to Buy Superluminescent Diodes

Super luminescent diodes our semiconductor devices which emit broadband optical radiation with a smooth spectrum. They can be used for optical coherence tomography (OCT), chromatic dispersion measurements, testing of optoelectronic components, and for some kinds of fiber-optic sensors.
