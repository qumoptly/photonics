Where to Buy Frequency Tripling Devices

Frequency tripling is achieved with frequency doubling and subsequent some frequency generation, and it usually leads to ultraviolet light. In addition to nonlinear crystals, a device may contain mounts, ovens, optical resonators, locking electronics, etc.

ESee also our encyclopedia article on frequency tripling!
Related articles: frequency doubling, frequency quadrupling, nonlinear frequency conversion, nonlinear crystal materials, ultraviolet lasers

Related products: frequency doubling devices, frequency quadrupling devices

Related product category: nonlinear optics

As part of our HarmoniXX product line, the HarmoniXX THG is a frequency converter for tripling the frequency of ultra short-pulse lasers.

Unlike conventional triplers, the HarmoniXX THG requires less adjustment efforts, because no separation and recombination of the interacting beams is needed. This user-friendly feature has one common optical beam path for all interacting beams. It is implemented by means of a proprietary delay compensator and provides consistent spatial overlap for optimum efficiency.
