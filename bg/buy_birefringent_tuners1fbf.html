Where to Buy Birefringent Tuners

Birefringent tuners are optical devices which are used mostly for wavelength tuning of lasers. There are single-plate and multi-plate designs, the latter typically exhibiting a much narrower filter bandwidth.

ESee also our encyclopedia article on birefringent tuners!
Related articles: optical filters, wavelength tuning, birefringence, Lyot filters

Related products: Lyot filters, wavelength tuning devices

Related product categories: optical components and devices, various devices
