Where to Buy Optical Choppers

Optical chopper devices can be used for periodically transmitting and interrupting a light beam. They are often used in conjunction with lock-in detection, but also for measuring fluorescence decay times and various other purposes.

ESee also our encyclopedia article on optical choppers!

Related products: optical modulators, intensity modulators

Related product categories: optical modulators, various devices
