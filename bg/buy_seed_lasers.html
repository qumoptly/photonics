Where to Buy Seed Lasers

Seed lasers are typically low-power lasers with good beam quality and low laser noise, providing inputs for high-power amplifier systems or injection-locked lasers.

ESee also our encyclopedia article on seed lasers!
Related articles: seed lasers, injection seeding, master oscillator power amplifier, master oscillator fiber amplifier

Related products: optical amplifiers, fiber amplifiers

Related product category: lasers and laser amplifiers
