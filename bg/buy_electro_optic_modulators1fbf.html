Where to Buy Electro-optic Modulators

Electro-optic modulators can be used as fast intensity or phase modulators, e.g. in laser devices, optical metrology and communications.

ESee also our encyclopedia article on electro-optic modulators!
Related articles: electro-optic effect, Pockels cells, pulse pickers, optical modulators, phase modulators, electroabsorption modulators, acousto-optic modulators

Related products: optical modulators, acousto-optic modulators, electroabsorption modulators

Related product categories: optical modulators, optical components and devices

Check List for Electro-optic Modulators

Click here to see a check list before buying such products!
  • Is the aperture sufficiently large for the laser beam?
  • Can the wanted modulation be achieved in terms of modulation index, bandwidth, etc.?
  • How about the optical losses?
  • For Q-switched lasers: is the damage threshold of the modulator high enough?
  • Is the switching sufficiently fast? Are there possibly disturbing ringing effects?
  • What modulator driver can I use? (What switching voltage is required?) How about cables and connectors?
  • Are the outer dimensions small enough to integrate the modulator into my optical setup?

In case that you need additional advice, consultancy from RP Photonics may be the solution.
