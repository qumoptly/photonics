Where to Buy Polarization Mode Dispersion Measurement and Compensation Equipment

Polarization mode dispersion can cause signal degradation in optical fiber communication systems. Therefore, it is important to have tools for measuring it and equipment for compensating it.

ESee also our encyclopedia article on polarization mode dispersion!
Related articles: fiber-optic links, polarization-maintaining fibers, birefringence

Related product category: detectors and measurements
