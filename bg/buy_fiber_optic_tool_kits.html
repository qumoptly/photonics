Where to Buy Fiber-optic Tool Kits

A toolkit can contain a range of equipment needed to work with fiber optics – for example, fiber connectors, adapters, tools for mounting connectors, coating strippers, knifes, tweezers, pliers, screwdrivers, rulers, materials like epoxy glue, and inspection tools like fiber end microscopes.
