Where to Buy Optical Surface Characterization Equipment

Many optical setups require parts with high-quality optical surfaces. Various kinds of optical surface characterization equipment are available, e.g. interferometers and surface profilers.

Related products: Twyman–Green interferometers, Fizeau interferometers, optical profilometers

Related product categories: detectors and measurements, optical components and devices

13 suppliers for optical surface characterization equipment are listed in the RP Photonics Buyer's Guide. Both manufacturers and distributors can be registered.
