Where to Buy Hyperspectral Imaging Instruments

Hyperspectral cameras do not only record images, but acquire a complete optical spectrum for each image point. They are used e.g. on airplanes and satellites for Earth monitoring, acquiring useful data e.g. for agricultural and environmental monitoring. They are also used for astronomical observations.

ESee also our encyclopedia article on hyperspectral imaging!

Related products: multispectral imaging instruments, cameras, Raman microscopes

Related product categories: detectors and measurements, applications, vision, displays and imaging
