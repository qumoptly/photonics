Where to Buy Optical Tables

Optical setups are often mounted on optical tables, which provide a high flexibility and are well isolated against vibrations. They usually have a regular area of threads, where the thread spacing and diameter can be based on metric or imperial units, e.g. with spacings of 25 mm or 1 inch.

ESee also our encyclopedia article on optical tables!

Related products: optical breadboards, optical mounts, opto-mechanics

Related product category: various devices
