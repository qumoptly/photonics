Where to Buy Terahertz Cameras

Terahertz cameras offer imaging based on terahertz radiation, which can e.g. penetrate clothes. A typical application is in airport security. Other applications are in medical imaging, spectroscopy and astronomy.

Related products: terahertz detectors, terahertz optics

Related product categories: detectors and measurements, vision, displays and imaging
