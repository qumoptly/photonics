Where to Buy Optical Sensors

Optical sensors are available for measuring many quantities like dimensions, distances, forces and pressures, strain and stress, deformations and temperatures. There are also distributed sensor systems, measuring such quantities e.g. along an optical fiber.

ESee also our encyclopedia article on optical sensors!
Related article: fiber-optic sensors

Related products: optical sensing instruments, fiber-optic sensors, laser rangefinders, optical time-domain reflectometers

Related product category: detectors and measurements
