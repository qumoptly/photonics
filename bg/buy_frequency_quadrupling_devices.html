Where to Buy Frequency Quadrupling Devices

Frequency quadrupling is achieved with two subsequent frequency doublers and generates ultraviolet light. The device may also contain crystal ovens, mounts, optical resonators, locking electronics, etc.

ESee also our encyclopedia article on frequency quadrupling!
Related articles: frequency doubling, frequency tripling, nonlinear frequency conversion, ultraviolet lasers

Related products: frequency doubling devices, frequency tripling devices

Related product category: nonlinear optics
