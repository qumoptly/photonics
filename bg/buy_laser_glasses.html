Where to Buy Laser Glasses

In contrast to laser crystals, laser glasses often exhibit a higher absorption and gain bandwidth, and they can be produced with large dimensions. Frequently used laser glasses are doped with neodymium, ytterbium or erbium ions.

ESee also our encyclopedia article on laser crystals versus glasses!
Related articles: laser crystals, rare-earth-doped gain media, transition-metal-doped gain media, neodymium-doped gain media, ytterbium-doped gain media, chromium-doped gain media, ceramic gain media

Related products: laser gain media, laser crystals, phosphate glasses

Related product category: materials
