Where to Buy Pulse Shapers

Pulse shapers are devices for manipulating the temporal and/or spectral shape of ultrashort laser pulses. For example, there are acousto-optic pulse shapers, operating on temporally stretched pulses, and dispersive pulse shapers with spatial light modulators.

ESee also our encyclopedia article on pulse shapers!

Related products: acousto-optic tunable filters

Related product categories: optical modulators, various devices
