Where to Buy Microscope Objectives

The microscope objective is a key component for reaching high performance of a microscope. Its main performance figures are the magnification and the numerical aperture, the latter limiting the image resolution.

ESee also our encyclopedia article on microscope objectives!
Related articles: lenses, focal length

Related products: microscopes, lenses

Related product categories: optical components and devices, vision, displays and imaging
