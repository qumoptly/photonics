Where to Buy Laser Modeling Software

There are software packages which serve as powerful tools for setting up certain laser models and working with them. They may address issues like the power conversion in laser gain media, modes of optical fibers and laser resonators, detrimental effects like thermal lensing and nonlinearities, and laser dynamics e.g. in the context of pulse generation and amplification.
