Where to Buy Chirped Mirrors

Chirped mirrors are a modified kind of Bragg mirrors, where the Bragg wavelength is systematically varied within the device. They can be ultra-broadband, and can be optimized for control chromatic dispersion over a wide wavelength range.

ESee also our encyclopedia article on chirped mirrors!
Related articles: chromatic dispersion, dispersion compensation, dispersive mirrors, dielectric mirrors

Related products: dispersive mirrors, dispersion compensation modules, laser mirrors, mirrors

Related product category: optical components and devices
