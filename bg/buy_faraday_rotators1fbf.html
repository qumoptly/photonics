Where to Buy Faraday Rotators

Faraday rotators can rotate the polarization state by 45°, and by another 45° when the beam is sent back. They can be used for eliminating polarization effects of components e.g. in lasers.

ESee also our encyclopedia article on Faraday rotators!
Related article: Faraday isolators

Related products: Faraday isolators, Faraday circulators

Related product category: optical components and devices
