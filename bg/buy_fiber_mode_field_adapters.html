Where to Buy Fiber Mode Field Adapters

Fiber mode field adapters can be used for efficiently coupling light from one fiber to another, even when they have substantially different mode sizes. They may be based on tapered fibers, for example.

ESee also our encyclopedia article on mode field converters!
Related articles: mode radius, effective mode area

Related products: fiber mode converters, tapered fibers

Related product categories: fiber optics, optical components and devices
