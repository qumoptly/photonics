Where to Buy Laser Gain Media

There is a wide range of laser gain media, including laser crystals, glasses and ceramics, gases and liquids. Most modern lasers are based on solid-state gain media, which come in different forms, such as bulk crystals and active optical fibers.

ESee also our encyclopedia article on gain media!
Related articles: laser crystals, rare-earth-doped gain media, transition-metal-doped gain media, ceramic gain media, laser crystals versus glasses

Related products: laser crystals, laser glasses, ceramic gain media, rare-earth-doped gain media, transition-metal-doped gain media
