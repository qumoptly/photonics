Where to Buy Fiber-optic Probes

Fiber-optic probes are tools for delivering and/or collecting light e.g. in spectroscopic measurements, imaging applications or in surgery. For example, they can be inserted brought into contact with biological specimen or inserted into cavities.

Related products: fibers, specialty fibers, fiber optics

Related product category: fiber optics
