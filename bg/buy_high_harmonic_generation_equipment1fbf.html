Where to Buy High Harmonic Generation Equipment

High harmonic generation is a process used to generate coherent light at extremely short wavelengths, sometimes reaching up to the soft X-ray region. The required equipment can include gas cells, focusing optics and dispersive devices for separating wavelength components.

ESee also our encyclopedia article on high harmonic generation!
Related articles: supercontinuum generation, nonlinear frequency conversion

Related product category: nonlinear optics
