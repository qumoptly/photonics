Where to Buy Diode-pumped Lasers

Various kinds of lasers, but mostly solid-stated lasers can be pumped with laser diodes. Compared with lamp-pumped lasers, they exhibit much higher power conversion efficiency and often also better beam quality and longer lifetimes.

ESee also our encyclopedia article on diode-pumped lasers!
Related articles: solid-state lasers, all-solid-state lasers, laser diodes, end pumping, side pumping

Related products: solid-state lasers

Related product category: lasers and laser amplifiers
